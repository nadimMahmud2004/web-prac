About The Author

Welcome to My World of Words

Elena Hart is an internationally acclaimed author whose works have been translated into 12 languages and have appeared on The New York Times Best Seller list multiple times. Born in London and educated at Oxford, she brings a unique perspective to contemporary fiction.

Her debut novel, "Whispers in the Dark," won the prestigious Booker Prize in 2015 and established her as a powerful new voice in literary fiction. Since then, she has published six novels and two collections of short stories.

When not writing, Elena teaches creative writing at Columbia University and leads workshops around the world. She lives in New York with her husband and two cats.
